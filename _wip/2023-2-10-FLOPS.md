## Supercomputers

In 2017, the best super computer was able to execute $$10.000.000.000.000~\text{FLOPS} = 10^{12}~\text{FLOPS} = 10~\text{TFLOPS}$$.

In general,

$$
\begin{equation}
    \text{FLOPS} = \text{cores} \times \frac{\text{cycles}}{\text{second}} \times \frac{\text{FLOPs}}{\text{cycle}}.
\end{equation}
$$

**100 TFLOPS is the goal for the next generation of single-chip supercomputers.**
Every floating point should be encoded with 64 bits.
