_Differential equations for emotions (or more generally: attraction and repulsion) between two or more agents are developed and studied which incorporate memory and noise. We discuss the resulting nonlinear dynamics and chaos for a wide variety of cases with possible applications to not just psychology and the social sciences but also economics._

# Introduction
I've been talking and writing about this a lot to friends as well as my girlfriend since she initially inspired me to think of this again early on when I fell in love with her. I was honestly scared that love can not possibly last forever. So I tried proving myself, mathematically, that it can in fact last forever. The equations I developed and studied turned out to be more general, beautiful, yet chaotic than I had expected.
